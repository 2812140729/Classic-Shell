Most programs play nice with windows (because its what they were designed to do) But not all programs play nice with other programs.
If you were to try and install 2 start menu programs, there would likely be some strange issues like maybe 2 overlapping start buttons (This is just an example of how 2, otherwise functioning programs 'interfere' with each-other)
Basically unless we know what program is causing the issue, we cant re-produce the issue, and without re-producing the issue, we cant make sure we solved it (we could probably make blind changes and HOPE it solves the issue, in your case. However that is likely to introduce more errors than it fixes)
