Post subject:  Re: Windows XP Luna
Correct. The skins can only change how the menu looks, not where the individual menu items appear. Achieving a 100% look of the XP menu is not possible with a skin. Also skins can't control the look per-item. So for example you can't have multiple fonts like the Internet Explorer and you can't have icons of different sizes in each column.

As for the items on the right side, you can edit them from the Customize Start Menu tab in the settings. You can have the exact same list as the one in your screenshot.
