Author:  Gaurav [ Thu Feb 21, 2013 10:10 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Classic Shell hangs off the Windows 7 process EXPLORER.E
Other users have had many troubles with various anti-virus programs and Classic Shell. Known ones to cause trouble with Classic Shell such as hangs and crashes are BitDefender and ZoneAlarm. On the problem PC, look for a whitelisting method to prevent blocking of specific EXEs in your anti-virus program and include C:\Windows\Explorer.exe and C:\Program Files\Classic Shell\ClassicStartMenu.exe. See if the issue goes away.
