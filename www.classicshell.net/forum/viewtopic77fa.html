Instead of wasting hours looking for image,

Info from another site: Dec 1, 2008

- To change the extension of the files inside the folder,
press Shift and right-click on the folder and select Open Command Window Here.
add this text " ren *.bin *.png " to the line, and push enter.
Now all the files inside the folder will change to the extension you want.

Now you try, or just go right to those image numbers.
