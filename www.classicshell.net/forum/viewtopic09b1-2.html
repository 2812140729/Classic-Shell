Experiment # 5 Out There.
Major changes made from Smoked Glass skin.

Misty here,
clear out there.

Yep. One of those iconic beach views.
Fake of course, almost like a cartoon.
The horizon will wander up and down somewhat. Not sure if anything can be done about that?
I still find it amazing that all these menus,
make most of themselves, using very efficient way of stretching!

Tried with CSM 4.0.2 on Win7/8. Autocolor background glass select, only works on Win8.
If required, change static color, in "personalize" feature, of Win7 .
Not compatible with most older versions of CSM. Will only partially work.
After downloading file, paste in skins folder. Exit CSM, restart CSM,
and go to "Start Menu Style" tab. Pick Classic or Classic two columns.
Pick "Out There" in Skin tab.

ps. Feel free to change anything in this skin, especially my errors,
as it's just an experiment. I'm no artist or coder, just playing around with stuff.
Sometimes errors can lead to new ideas.
In this case, the vertical noise problem in the making of a previous skin,
gave me the idea for using it here.
So, there is a lot of streaking of carefully selected one pixel wide noise,
mostly for the fake waves.
Nothing is permanent, everything changes.
You could make it better. French doors, light to dark sand, sunset, moon glow, etc.
Just noticed 4.0.4 is out, Hopefully still works.
