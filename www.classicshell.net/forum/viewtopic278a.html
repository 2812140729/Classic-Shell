You can fix the text of the Shutdown button. Open C:\Program Files\Classic Shell\StartMenuL10N.ini as administrator.

Find the line under en-US (English) that says:
Menu.Shutdown = &Shut Downaaaliiiiiigi

and change it to:
Menu.Shutdown = Sh&ut Down
