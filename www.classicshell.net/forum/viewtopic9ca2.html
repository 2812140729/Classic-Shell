1) Right clicking on the Start button shows the context menu with 'Windows Explorer', 'Open', 'Open All Users', 'Settings', 'Help' and 'Exit' items. If instead you are getting the Win+X menu, then try Shift+right click. There is a setting on the Controls tab "Right click opens Win X menu" to control this.

2) You may have reduced the main menu and/or sub-menus widths which is why you are seeing '...'. Go to the "Menu Look" tab and increase the widths or right click the values and click "Set default".
