Hi SebastianOnofrei4454

If you mean the dark line around button, that's the design.
If what you see is a big black background on the taskbar,
that means the original bitmap with alpha was not copied properly.

Each browser can work differently.
Saving an image from webpage might come out as something else.
If possible instead of saving, use copy and paste function.
Then the black background will be invisible when it's used as a button in program.



For easability, I converted those buttons from transparent bitmaps, to more compatable transparent png.
They are in the zip.
