Post subject:  Re: App's Icons in Taskbar are Greyed out if also in start
I see. In your case the pinned programs are stored in the "Start Menu" folder. It appears that the latest version of the taskbar is not liking that.
First unpin the apps from the start menu.
Then go to the Main Menu tab in the settings, and switch the pinned programs from "Use Start Menu folder" to "Use Pinned folder".
Finally re-pin your programs.
