In Start Menu Settings, check if "Start automatically for this user" is enabled on General Behavior tab.

If the menu is not starting, copy and paste a shortcut to C:\Program Files\Classic Shell\ClassicStartMenu.exe in your Startup folder at C:\Users\<your user name>\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\Startup
