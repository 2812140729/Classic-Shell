One way is to press Win+F. It will open the Metro search for files.
Another is to type search-ms: in the Win+R box. It will open Explorer in search mode.
Then you can click on the icon in the address bar and drag it to the desktop. This will create a shortcut you can later click to open the search.
Once you have a shortcut you can give it a hotkey (like Ctrl+Alt+F)
