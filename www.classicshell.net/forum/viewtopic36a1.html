Post subject:  Re: How do i delete these items?
Look at the Setting: Shutdown menu items on Main Menu tab. Hover over it to read its tooltip help. For that setting, you need to specify the commands you wish to show in the Shutdown menu. The ones you omit will be hidden. The possible commands are: switch_user, windows_security, logoff, lock, disconnect, undock, restart, sleep, hibernate, shutdown. Separate the commands with a comma or semicolon. The order of commands is not important.
