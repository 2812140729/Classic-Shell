If you are talking about the "recent programs" list in the classic start menu, then you can disable it from the "Special Items" tab in the settings. Look for a setting called "Show recent programs" near the bottom.

To enable tracking of recent documents, right-click on the taskbar and select Properties. In the Start Menu tab make sure that the setting "Store and display recently opened items" is checked.
