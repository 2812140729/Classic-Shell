Post subject:  Re: Floating startmenu skin
clrSharp2 Glass revB.skin has menu floating at bottom.


The classic Metro.skin could be modified to have it float.

Increase menu bitmap size to include invisible portion (0 alpha)
Increase the bitmap dimension slices to match new size.
Increase main padding, to push the text further down.
Increase padding for name /user picture.

Here is simple example Metro Float.skin to learn from, and personalize your way.
Only two column has float option.
I did not test every funtion. function.
I did not check for spelling mistakes.
