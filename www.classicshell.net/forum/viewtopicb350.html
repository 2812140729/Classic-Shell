SebastianOnofrei4454, I suggest you learn about transparency in images and how to use the right image editor to correctly edit transparent images. In all of your postings, I have observed you have converted 32-bit BMPs to 24-bit BMPs. Also, while it's OK to modify others' content as long as you acknowledge it is based on their work, please do it properly. For example, resized buttons must not be pixelated, nor severely degraded in quality. They must not be missing their transparency and should not be exactly the same as the original poster. Try to learn about anti-aliasing too to smooth out the rough edges. I suggest an easy to use, free editor like Paint.net with a 32-bit BMP plugin to open BMP images with transparency (http://forums.getpaint.net/index.php?/t ... -paintnet/). You can also save as PNG.
