Classic Shell's Start Menu supports bitmap elements in skins and flat colors. It does not support gradients between two colors yet as of version 4.1.0. That's why the "title bar" of the menu can't follow window title bar colors. You can include a bitmap with a gradient but it won't follow system title bar colors.

To open Frequent/Recent programs by keyboard accelerator (first letter), go to the Main Menu tab in Start Menu settings and look for the setting called "Shortcuts for the recent programs". Set it to "Normal shortcuts" and click OK.
