Post subject:  Windows 8.1: Don't show regular start menu upon signing in
On Windows 8.1, Classic Shell fixes its awkward tablet-style menu when you click on the start button, but when you turn on the computer and sign in to your account, the awkward menu still shows up right after. It only happens upon signing in, but it's still a little bothersome.

Could you guys make it so that after signing in, you'd be immediately sent to your desktop rather than having to go through the regular windows 8.1 menu first?
Author:  Gaurav [ Fri May 26, 2017 9:03 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Windows 8.1: Don't show regular start menu upon signing   
Right click Taskbar and go to Navigation tab. Check "When I sign in or close all apps on a screen, go to the Desktop instead of Start."
