@Bowserb, on your laptop, Switch from "Recent" to "Frequent" list in Classic Start Menu settings. Then right click your Taskbar -> Properties. Go to the 2nd tab and check the option "Store recently opened programs". Thereafter programs started from Quick Launch etc will also start getting listed in the Start Menu as you use them.
