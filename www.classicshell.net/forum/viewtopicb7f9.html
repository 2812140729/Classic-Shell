Author:  Ivo [ Tue Dec 23, 2014 8:06 am ]
Post subject:  Re: Classic Style - Downloads as Link, and Apps Folder
To make the Downloads a simple link, check the "Don't expand" setting for that menu item.

To remove the Apps menu item, simply delete it.

Post subject:  Re: Classic Style - Downloads as Link, and Apps Folder  
You need to delete Apps from the "Customize Start Menu" tab - the same place you are adding the Downloads folder.
Either right-click -> Delete Item, or select and press Del, or simply drag it away.
