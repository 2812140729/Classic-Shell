Googleized Opaque [7 style] for CSM 4.3.0

I borrowed the buttons, etc from the screenshot of Google image search.
It's kind of dark. Might not be useful on some LCD displays,
especially if the taskbar is at the top. The selector button could be difficult to see.
I made the hover text color white to help discern.
There are no white submenus in this skin. They are banned.


EDIT: Ok, now it's revB.

Most horizontal separators can butt up against the vertical line.


This is the only way that I know of by extending the separators further than the bitmaps padding.
Maybe some simply worded text and - number? I bet Ivo knows.
There had to be some compromises along the way, to achieve that look, as it's a sneaky way to do it.
Like the small offset search box button, vertically offset arrows and spaces in between 1st column entries.
The submenu offset(part of the submenu that lays on top of selector button), is chaotic, because of too many variables going on.

The option can be selectively turned off.

I thought adding all the extra button images with extra invisible padding, would increase the file size a lot. Nope.
Due to no texture, and using good old png, skin file size is 90 KB.
