Hi Redblade.
Implemented the Trick.
Download the .zip file and there is bitmap- Emblem1_mask.
Open it with MS Paint.
Add the text you want in the red boxes by Using [A] Command.

Note1:- Write With GREEN Color. I mean text should be green. By green, I mean 255 Green Value Or RGB-00FF00.

Note2:- Try to keep labels small. Text size should not be greater than about 11. Otherwise, labels get outside from tile as well.

Note3:- No need of old Tile Text.png and Tile Background.bmp

Caution1:- As before, don't move boxes here and there.

Drawback:- There is a little drawback of new trick. Alignment. You yourself have to move the textbox to right place.

After done, load the skin file, There is option for chosing emblem mask, point to the file.
Other Options, Your Choices.
In this revision, most of the options are merged together.
