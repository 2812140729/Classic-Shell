They can work without the theme in Windows 10. There won't be text glow.

Menu looks, are totally different between operating systems, some pleasant, some not.
Win 10 will boost the glass color and opacity blur.

To reduce the residual non neutral glass color on Win10, do any, or all of these adjustments:
In Windows personalize settings-pick a dark grey color
In CSM settings-reduce "Glass opacity" in "menu Look" tab.
In CSM settings-change "Override glass color" in "menu Look" tab, to a dark grey/black.
In skin settings- turn on "Reduce Glass Color"

To stop brief glass color flash around menu, turn off "Main menu animation" in "Menu Look" tab.

I could never figure out how to make it look good with black text and text shadow.

You can go into file, and edit for darker or lighter text shadow:

[GLASS_SHADOW AND BLACK_TEXT]
Main_text_shadow_color=#D0D0D0,#D0D0D0,#FFFFFF,#FFFFFF

to shadow lighter
Main_text_shadow_color=#FFFFFF,#FFFFFF,#FFFFFF,#FFFFFF



Same with white text:

[GLASS_SHADOW]
Main_text_shadow_color=#202020,#202020,#000000,#000000

to shadow darker
Main_text_shadow_color=#000000,#000000,#000000,#000000
