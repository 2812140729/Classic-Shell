You can already pin anything to the Start Menu by dragging it over the Start button, wait for the Classic menu to open and drop it in the pinned area. Or you can right click shortcuts or EXEs and choose "Pin to Start Menu (Classic Shell)".

See this trick to organize items into groups: viewtopic.php?f=7&t=3403

Although the space is limited vertically in the Classic menu to pin a large number of items without reducing the items shown in the Recent/Frequent list.
