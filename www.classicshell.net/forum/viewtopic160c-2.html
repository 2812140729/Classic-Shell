Download the Classic Shell Utility from the bottom of this page: http://www.classicshell.net/downloads/
Run it, then choose the option to manually uninstall. It may ask you to tell it where the installation path was, for example C:\Program Files\Classic Shell.
Follow the prompts, then restart.
