Post subject:  Re: Toolbar issue Windows 10
Open Control Panel and press F10. Right click on the Menu bar (File, Edit......) and uncheck "Classic Explorer Bar".

Another way using the Ribbon UI of Explorer is to go to the "View" tab of the Ribbon. Click the tiny arrow below the Options button and uncheck "Classic Explorer Bar".
