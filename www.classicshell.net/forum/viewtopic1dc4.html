Author:  Ivo [ Tue Jan 22, 2013 7:14 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Screen won't reawaken
There are 3 possibilities - the problem may not be related to Classic Shell, and it is just a coincidence. The problem may be related to the start menu. The problem may be related to the service.
You can stop the start menu by right-clicking on the start button and selecting Exit. See if the problem persists.
You can stop the service from services.msc and see if that fixes the problem.
