Author:  Gaurav [ Thu Mar 10, 2016 7:29 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Recent documents menu on Windows 10 is always empty
Recent Items list is still maintained by Windows, it is only shown by Classic Shell. Go to the Settings app in Windows 10 (Win key+i) > Personalization > Start and turn on "Show recently opened items in Jump Lists on Start or the taskbar".
