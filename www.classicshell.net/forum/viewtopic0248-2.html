Author:  Jcee [ Sat Nov 14, 2015 11:31 am ]
Post subject:  Re: classic to explore is to the left
Go to the control panel (because it has no Ribbon UI) then right click the blank space and unlock the toolbar (if its locked) Then drag classic explorer toolbar to the right, and lock it again.
It should remain, even outside of the control panel
Alternatively you could use 2 or more toolbars.
