Right mouse click on your start orb, click settings, select Taskbar label, click Customize taskbar to enable the changes, then go to Taskbar texture, click at the end of the line, browse to the folder where you saved your taskbar image to load.
As a side note, you may need to tweak Horizontal and Vertical stretching, using Adapt.
