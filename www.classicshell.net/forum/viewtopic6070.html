Hi MisterMoo.
You must be the first person who ever did not want icons.
That is very easy to turn off in Classic Shell menu settings, under the "Menu look" tab.
Just change the "Small icon size" and "Large icon size" to 0
Exit CSM, restart CSM.
Now there won't be any icons on main, or submenus, on any skin.

I know you want Jcee to help make your ultimate shiny skin,
but Splitwirez one looks quite Applelish for the mean time.
