[Animated Start Button] Spore Rotate

The spokes rotate a bit, less than a second in length.

With limited knowledge, and software, I used basic ways to rotate the spokes.
Took a while to figure it all out.
Separated the spokes from the background,
so it can be rotated separately on a new layer in image program.

Next, it's all math.
It has 5 spokes for 360 degrees.
To rotate a spoke to next position equals 72 degrees.
To get smooth movement, made it rotate 6 degrees each time.
So 12 times is where one spoke got to next spoke position, 1/5 rotation.
I duplicated those frames again to get total of 2/5 rotation.

If user hovers, & aborts menu, spokes rotate backwards to a little bounce at endd.

On Win 10, I have button size at 42
