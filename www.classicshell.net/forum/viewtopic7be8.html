Author:  Ivo [ Sat Mar 11, 2017 9:46 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: How does CSM obtain applications' Jump List information?
Classic Shell uses the undocumented interfaces IDestinationList and IAutomaticDestinationList. Keep in mind that the GUIDs for those interfaces are different depending on the version of Windows, as well as the interfaces themselves may be different.
