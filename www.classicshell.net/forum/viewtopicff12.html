Author:  Gaurav [ Sat Oct 31, 2015 5:51 am ]
Post subject:  Re: Icons size in Explorer on Windows 10 - Details view
You can increase your system DPI. Type: dpi into the Classic Shell Menu search, open DPIScaling.exe. Click "Set a custom scaling level" and set it above 100%. On Windows 10, you can also change it from the Settings app. Then log out and log in again. Your Explorer Details/Table view items should be larger.
