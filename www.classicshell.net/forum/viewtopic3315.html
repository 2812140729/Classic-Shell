that shouldnt happen, definently a bug; If its for 1-2 specific games, try the following;

toggle between fullscreen and fullscreen(window) a change like this may make the bug dissapear, Just note fullscreen(window) generally suffers from a 3-5% fps performance hit (it also allows you to very quickly minimize/maximize)
Toggle DX 12 mode. its hard to say exactly what combination of hardware/drivers/ and OS version is causing the issue in your specific case. (there are other similarly reported cases, but they have been a constant, and IVO has never been able to pin exactly what was causing it down)


Alternatively; try toggling the 'replace start button' setting in classic shell. sometimes this bug would occur only with the default button, other times only with the custom button. sometimes with both.
