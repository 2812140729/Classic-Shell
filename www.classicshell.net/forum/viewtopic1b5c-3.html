Post subject:  Re: ClassicShell do not seem to work
Right click the Start button and click Settings. Make sure "Left Click opens" and "Windows Key opens" are both set to open the "Classic Start Menu" and not the "Windows Start Menu". These settings are on the "Controls" tab which appears when "Show all settings" checkbox is checked at the top of the Settings window.

Also, press Win+A and make sure that Tablet Mode is turned off.
