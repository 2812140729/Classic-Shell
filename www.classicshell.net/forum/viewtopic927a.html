The thing is; by default windows 7-10 should all provide this feature; so it is unlikely that classic shell would ever attempt to implement it (if we even decided to venture further into tweaking the taskbar) and there is an issue on your system preventing windows default behavior from behaving how it should.

If you updated 3 weeks ago. you are likely on the fall creators update (V1709)
If this is the case, fall creators update was JUST released (right about 3 weeks ago) and is quite buggy at the moment (I'm experiencing several crashes myself, unrelated to classic shell)
You can contact microsoft with your specific issue, to increase the likelihood of the problem being discovered (otherwise you can wait for someone else to find/report it)

Alternatively; if any number other than 'Version 1709' is displayed, you can download updates; which may or may not solve your issue (or cause other issues, as like i said Fall creators update is quite buggy ATM)
