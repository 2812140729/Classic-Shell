Go to the Program Files\Classic Shell folder and click on the shortcut "Start Menu Settings". Then go through all the pages and look at all settings. The ones that have non-default values will be shown in bold. Try to find the one that is breaking things. You can also reset all settings from the Backup button at the bottom.
