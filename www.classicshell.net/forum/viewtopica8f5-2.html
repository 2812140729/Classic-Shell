Post subject:  Re: Can anyone figure out why my RADIOGROUP isn't displaying
Whatever option names you put in the radio group line,
they have to be defined as options below it,
and make sure to have one option as default on ",1 " with the rest of the group options as ",0 "



OPTION RADIOGROUP="Numbers",0,11|22|33
OPTION 11="Eleven",0
OPTION 22="Twenty Two (My option shows when skin used for first time, or skin is reset)",1
OPTION 33="Thirty Three",0


Check out skin tutorial, or better yet, another yet skin.
I see it's skin7. If it were Classic 1/2, then those options would also need to be put in lines

Classic1_options=11,22,33
Classic2_options=11,22,33
