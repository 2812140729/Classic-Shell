This is a compilation of (almost) all the skins posted in this Sub-forum;

It is posted simply for convenience, and If you find something you like or have feedback about a specific skin, please locate the original post (in thissub-forum) and respond there. I have not personally modified, or own the rights to Most of these skins. If you created any of them and have an objection with this Compilation; Simply Message me (or post below) with the name(s) of the skins in question; and I will remove them. This compilation has been (quickly) sifted through; and I removed about 15 skins; I deemed as either older versions of an included skin, or too broke to function. Many of the skins here are rough around the edges; but should at-least function.
To use, simply unzip and then either: Merge it with your 'skins' folder, or replace it (renaming the old one, to keep a backup) Which is located in classic shells install folder. ( "C:\Program Files\Classic Shell" In most cases)
