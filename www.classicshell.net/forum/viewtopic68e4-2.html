It can be uninstalled from Control Panel -> Programs and Features. There is no requirement to use Revo Uninstaller or third party tools to remove it. After you remove it, it returns Windows to how it was, reversing all its effects, only the settings will be left in the Registry at HKCU\Software\IvoSoft.
