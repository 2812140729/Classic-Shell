Author:  Jcee [ Wed Dec 27, 2017 6:56 am ]
Post subject:  Re: Transfer to new computer
Yes, the easiest way is to right click your start button go to settings, then at the bottom you will see 'backup'
Save to .xml
then copy that to a flash drive (or email or whatever) and use it on your new system
NOTE: this will not save any 3rd party .skin files, taskbar backgrounds, or start buttons. (you have to manually transfer those too)
