Author:  Gaurav [ Sat Jan 19, 2013 12:14 am ]
Post subject:  Re: W8: Windows Update
In the search box, type 'Windows Update' and right click it, then click 'Create shortcut'. The shortcut will be created on the desktop. Now right click the Start button, click Open and move the shortcut from the desktop there.
