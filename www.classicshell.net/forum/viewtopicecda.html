It wasn't the antivirus. I've managed to solve it by looking into the Event viewer and noticing errors related to IDTNC64.cpl. After a quick search on Google I found that this file can cause Explorer to crash on Win10. I renamed it to IDTNC64.cpl.bak as suggested on another forum and then I installed Classic Shell: the issue is gone, now everything works properly!
