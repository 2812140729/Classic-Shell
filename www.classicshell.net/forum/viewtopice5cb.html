Author:  Ivo [ Sat Nov 16, 2013 12:05 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Skin7 transparency (resource hacker).  
No. This is not supported. The reason is that the area is used as a background for the programs tree, which is a system control. System controls don't work with transparent backgrounds. So basically "fullglass" and "fullalpha" are not allowed for the main menu.
