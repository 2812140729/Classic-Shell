No, there is no problem using IE10 with Classic Shell. Classic IE9 means that it will work with IE9 or greater ! You can safely upgrade to IE10. FYI: I am running IE11 without problems!

But you should consider to upgrade to a newer version of Classic Shell. I am currently running 3.9.5 RC on Windows 8.1, no problems so far!
Author:  Gaurav [ Tue Sep 17, 2013 8:13 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Going from IE9 to IE10
You can upgrade without uninstalling as the IE addons work with IE9+. If after upgrading they don't work, then re-enable them again from Internet Options -> Manage Addons. You may lose your saved tabs/loaded pages in IE during the upgrade.
