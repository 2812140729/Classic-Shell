Author:  Gaurav [ Sun Apr 12, 2015 8:16 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Icon size
There are two ways to change the icon size.

1) Either check the "Small icons" option on the Skin tab in Start Menu settings. This will turn main menu icons small.
2) Or go to the Menu Look tab and change "Small icon size" and "Large icon size" values. "The "Small icon size" is used for submenus and "Large icon size" is used for main menu.
