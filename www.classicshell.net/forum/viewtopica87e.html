Author:  Gaurav [ Wed Feb 06, 2013 5:15 am ]
Post subject:  Re: View in Control Panel
So you want to change it to Category view? Open Settings, go to Customize Start Menu tab. Double click ControlPanelItem in left column and change "Command" from control_panel to control_panel_categories from the dropdown.
