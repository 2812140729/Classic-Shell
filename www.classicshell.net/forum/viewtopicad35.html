Author:  Gaurav [ Fri Sep 30, 2016 8:21 am ]
Post subject:  Re: how do i display classic explorer settings?
Start -> Programs -> Classic Shell -> Classic Explorer Settings. Or you can search for: classic explorer settings. Another way is to right click the toolbar it adds to Explorer and choose Classic Explorer Settings. You can also assign a hotkey to the shortcut in the Start Menu e.g. Ctrl+Shift+X. Then pressing it will directly open the shortcut.
