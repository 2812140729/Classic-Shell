Author:  juniper7 [ Sat Aug 20, 2016 2:29 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Windows 10 Start Menu With Emblem Tiles
Masks.

Kind of like using masking tape, when painting.
You put the masking tape, where you don't want the paint to go.
But it works out the opposite here. Wherever the mask is,
that's where an assigned color, or image shows up, in the menu.

It's a bit like TV and movies have a color mask, blue chroma key, or green screen, detected in a narrow color range,
and replaced with fake scenery behind actor, weatherman.

Ivo has made available different color masks, (you don't see that color on the menu on desktop),
that can be used for having masks show system glass color, Metro colors, menu color, photo images, tints.
The masks can be blended together for gradient mixing effects.

There is a skinning tutorial describing in better detail.

http://classicshell.net/tutorials/skintutorial.html
