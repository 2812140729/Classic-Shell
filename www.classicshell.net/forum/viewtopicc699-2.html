If the focus is in the search box when you open the menu, only the first time you need to press Alt+accelerator. The next time, press only C to take it to the next item with C. I know it's a bit confusing - having to use Alt sometimes and only the accelerator key at other times. I have always felt that it should be one consistent keystroke regardless of search box focus like F1, F2, ...., F12.
