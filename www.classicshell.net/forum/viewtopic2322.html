UPDATE:

Got it back to normal!
Seems like the toolbar still existed since I had other available toolbars to use. In this case, Adobe Acrobat & Norton Toolbar.
I had to disable these options in Internet Options -> Programs -> Manage add-ons, then had to ensure Lock the toolbars was active.

Just in case anyone runs into the same issue.
