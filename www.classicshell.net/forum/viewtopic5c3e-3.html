Post subject:  Re: Please help me,,
Classic Shell may not be loading at startup. Just create a shortcut to C:\Program Files\Classic Shell\ClassicStartMenu.exe and copy-paste it in your Startup folder at C:\Users\<your user name>\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\Startup. Next time you shutdown, it will load automatically.
