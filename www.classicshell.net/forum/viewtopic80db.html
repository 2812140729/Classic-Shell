Post subject:  Shift+enter to open menu items instead of expanding.
Currently you can double-click an expanding menu item to open it in an explorer window (very handy for getting to a folder quickly). To do this with the keyboard you have to press the menu key followed by O, which is slower.

Since you can press enter to expand the item normally, shift+enter would seem like a suitable way to open it instead.
