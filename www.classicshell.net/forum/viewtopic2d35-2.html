Off White System [Classic skin] Works on CSM version 4.3.0

It's a system looking menu.
I copied the light grey colors from Windows 10 Explorer, Edge, & an appy.

If you see a purple, blue, or green tint, it is color shift in your Lcd monitor.
The only colored part of menu, is aqua blue new programs selector.
This menu has no glass color, no Metro color, no blur transparency, and no gradients.
It could be called, I guess, Flat Vanilla? or Vanilla Minilla Godzilla
