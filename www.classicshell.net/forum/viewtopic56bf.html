Author:  Gaurav [ Tue Jun 21, 2016 11:49 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Not seeing thumbnails
Classic Shell does not affect Explorer thumbnails in any way. To verify that, you can try uninstalling Classic Shell temporarily.

Press F10 or Alt key to show the menu bar. From Tools menu -> Folder Options -> View tab, you can check "Always show menus". Also uncheck "Always show icons, never thumbnails" and see if your thumbnail issue is fixed.
