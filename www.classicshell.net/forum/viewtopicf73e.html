You can set the size for "small icons" and "large icons". The sub-menus always use the small icons. The main menu uses small icons or large icons depending on the skin. Almost all skins have a setting you can toggle.
So - set the small icons to a small number, se the large icons to a larger number, then look in the skin settings for something called "Small icons" and uncheck it.
