That's a jumplist and it's not part of Classic Shell, it's part of Windows. You must be using a third party visual style (theme) which is why it looks like that. Themes (visual styles) must exactly match the OS version for which they were designed. e.g. Windows 8 themes won't look right on Windows 7. Windows 8.1 themes won't look right on Windows 8 either. Even Windows 8.1 Update 1 requires themes to be updated.
