Author:  Jcee [ Fri Apr 27, 2018 1:39 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Problem with search - pc-settings
Search your screen for the start button, and go to Settings
Then click the searchbox at top and type 'search'
Then search the 11 displayed search results related to the text 'search'
and check any you want your searches to search.
Then press OK
and Search.

Hopefully, this helps (most of these settings are checked by default tho) so if-not it might be a bug caused by windows 10 (and without classic shell updates, its unlikely to get fixed)
