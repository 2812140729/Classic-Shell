It does work on Win7. Did come with browser data captureware.
Theme didn't work on Win 10 either.

It would probably be better to ask on a theme site,
as to what extra info to put in the msstyle files resource folder,
as well as use an alternative compatible Win7 menu program,
that works exactly from theme files, possibly Startisback.

The toughest would be to make into a CSM skin.
Looking at the curves on the top of the Batman menu,
means the horizontal width has to be locked in to exact pixel amount, to avoid stretching.
Ivo has put in a couple of settings that could work with that.
The blue text glow won't work in 8/10.

It is possible for a basic menu skin.
I wouldn't mind trying later.
Only after I finish with conversion of one of my Win7style skins, back to classic style.
