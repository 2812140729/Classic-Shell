Author:  Ivo [ Thu Aug 06, 2015 8:16 am ]
Post subject:  Re: There is any skin that match win10 blur style ?
Every skin decides how much of the glass color to use and how to mix it with its own color. On one end you have the "full glass" skin which uses the glass color at almost 100%, and on the other you have "metro" and "midnight", which ignore the glass color almost completely.
It is designed to make all of the skin elements be readable and with enough contrast.

In this example, the "metro" skin uses the system metro colors for its elements - background, text, selection, various highlights. Those colors are picked to work well together. Changing the background color arbitrarily will make them not match anymore. For example if you pick a white color the text wont be readable.
