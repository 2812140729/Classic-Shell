Author:  Gaurav [ Tue Jan 15, 2013 12:48 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: MOVING APPS IN START MENU
App shortcuts are located here: %localappdata%\Microsoft\Windows\Application Shortcuts inside a folder each. By default, just left click drag and drop creates a shortcut. You can hold down Shift and left click and drag and drop them to move them inside 'All Programs' or subfolders inside 'All Programs' or hold down Ctrl while dragging to copy them.
