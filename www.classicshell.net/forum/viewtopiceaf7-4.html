Post subject:  Re: Full ClearType (color) subpixel anti-aliasing in IE10
IE9/IE10/IE11 use DirectWrite to render text and Microsoft has offered no control over the ClearType implementation in DirectWrite, not even an IE specific setting. There were app-specific hacks by some developers (http://blog.softwareninjas.ca/2011/03/how-to-take-control-of-internet.html) for IE9/apps that used IE9's renderer that involved intercepting all calls to DWrite.dll. Those hacks may need to be updated for IE10/IE11.
