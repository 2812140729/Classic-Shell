Author:  Ivo [ Tue Jan 08, 2013 12:22 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Cant move files
You can try a tool like Process Explorer, that can search for open handles to specific files. A likely suspect for keeping files locked is AV software.

There are ways to disable Classic Shell, but they are different for each component. The easiest is to just uninstall it. Your settings will be preserved for the next time you install.
