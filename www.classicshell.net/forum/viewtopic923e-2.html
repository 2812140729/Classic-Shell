Post subject:  Re: How can I get to C:\...and a software's files?
The easiest way:
Hold the windows key down and press the E key: [win]+e
From there type C: into the address bar at the top

Through the default start menu:
Hit the start button, and go to "File Explorer" on the bottom left corner

Through Classic shell:
right click the start button, and select 'windows explorer'
OR
Depending on what style/setting Look for 'My computer' or 'This PC' after left clicking the start menu
