Post subject:  Re: Uninstall 3rd Party App
No, right click on Start button -> Settings will open Classic Shell Settings which are different from Windows Settings.

To uninstall an app in Windows 10, press Win+i keys together on your keyboard to open the Windows Settings app. From there, the rest of the instructions you posted are correct. Go to System - Apps & features. Select the app and click Uninstall.

Another way to uninstall an app when Classic Shell is installed is to go to Start -> All Programs -> Apps folder. Locate your app and right click it -> Uninstall.
