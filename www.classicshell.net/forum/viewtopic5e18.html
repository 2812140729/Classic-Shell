Guess it's a personal thing, Splitwirez.
It does look nice. If it was real, I would want it a little more subtle.
Some animated effects look great at the start, like fireworks, then one tires of seeing effect after a number of times.
Other effects look nice and one does not tire of.

Kind of like sounds.
At one job I had, production manager asked me to make a time bell for the break times,
throughout staggered 2 shifts. Each day went off 25 times.
Seems bad, but when you are concentrating on your work, one tends to forget to look what time it is.
Then miss the coffee truck.


I tried many sounds. There was only one sound, (vibraphone chime with different pitches/tempo),
that could be played throughout the day, that was pleasant to listen to.
If I had used any other sound, people would have complained to shut it down the second day.
It lasted for over 5 years before company finally bought a professional all in one system.

Remember when hundreds of transition effects came out for video/slide shows?
Most just get too distracting. People had more effects than the content.
Its better when effect randomly happens, or subtle.

How about that for a comment. Next time gonna be just one word.
