Author:  Ivo [ Tue May 21, 2013 9:55 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Charms Bar
You have to determine why it shows up.
It can be because you move the mouse to the top-right or bottom-right corner. If so, set the "Disable active corners" setting to All.
It can be because you are hitting the trackpad of your laptop. Then you have to look in the trackpad driver settings for a way to disable the gestures.
Author:  Gaurav [ Tue May 21, 2013 10:06 pm ]
Post subject:  Re: Charms Bar
First open Classic Start Menu settings, enable All Settings and go to the 'Windows 8 Settings' tab. Set the Disable active corners setting to All. This will disable the Charms for the mouse.If it still pops up after doing this, then it may be because of your laptop's touchpad drivers. Look in the Mouse Control Panel to see if there is a way to disable the touch gestures - it is very likely that there will be a setting there to disable it.
