"settings for start menu"
right click your classic shell button go to settings
then click all settings on the bottom, then go to available commands, and find "menu settings" drag that over to your current menu items, wherever you want it to be on the list.

"classic shell in all programs"
click the button, and right click programs then select 'open'
paste a shortcut to your classic shell folder there, (or create a new folder, with all the shortcuts to whichever items from the folder u want to display, then create a shortcut to the new folder)
