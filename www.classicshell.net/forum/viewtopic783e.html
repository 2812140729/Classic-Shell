Author:  Gaurav [ Tue Jul 02, 2013 8:42 am ]
Post subject:  Re: Who can help me to made my own Button??
Please do as follows:
1. Right click the Start button and open Settings.
2. Go to the "Start Button" tab.
3. Choose the "Classic" button, not the "Custom" button. This is because you said you wanted the classic old style button.
4. Click on 'Button icon' (not button image). Then click the [...] next to it.
5. Now select the icon by browsing to it and point it to this .ICO file: http://www.classicshell.net/forum/download/file.php?id=376 . (You will have to first extract the icon from the ZIP file.)
6. Click OK twice.
