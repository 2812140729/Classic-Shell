Do you mean the black background context menu that makes you feel that high contrast is turned on only for a part of the UI? The larger menu with bigger spacing will make it not fit the height of most screens so the menu will need to have pagers for scrolling at the top and bottom. The context menu for items is full of commands that barely fit the height of the screen with the current style.
