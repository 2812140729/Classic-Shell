The menu that shows recently opened documents is called "Documents" in the classic menu and "Recent Items" in the Windows 7 menu. Why they renamed it to Recent Items I don't know. But it still shows only documents.

The "RecentItems" item contains "the menu items recently launched from the start menu". For something to end up there it has to be launched from a menu with the "track recent items" enabled. Most people don't need to deal with RecentItems. It is for people who want to separate the recent programs in a sub-menu because their main menu is getting too big.
