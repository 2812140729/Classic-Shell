Step by step from the jpg

1 dowload latest stable https://www.gimp.org/downloads/

2 right click the image, and select open with find gimp

3 OPTIONAL Go to Image>Scale image and make the height 786, allowing the width to scale accordingly
4 OPTIONAL Go to Image>Canvas size and crop the image to 666 width

5 File>Export as then type WhateverName.bmp
6 Check Compatibility Options: 'Do not Write color space information'
7 Check Advanced Options: "16 Bit X1 R5 G5 B5" Then click Export
8 Locate this image in explorer, right click and select edit (This should open it in MS PAINT, otherwise go to open with, and find PAINT)
9. Hit the Save icon then exit paint
10. Locate your image in reshacker, and replace resource 31 then save

Main_text_color=#000000,#000000,#9F9F9F,#AFAFAF

Submenu_text_color=#000000,#000000,#7F7F7F,#7F7F7F
Submenu_arrow_color=#000000,#000000
Main_selection=20
Submenu_selection=20
Main_split_selection=21
Submenu_split_selection=21
Main_split_selection2=21
Submenu_separator=24
Submenu_separatorV=25
Main_separator=24





Anything with 'text_color' affects various text colors..
